Plant hardiness zones reconsidered!

Moving beyond standard USDA hardiness maps to better understand typical conditions in US locations.

Zone designations based on absolute minimum temperature don't provide sufficient information for plant species survival probabilities for a particular region. Hourly trends and total time below thresholds can be important factors in selecting species for cultivation.
